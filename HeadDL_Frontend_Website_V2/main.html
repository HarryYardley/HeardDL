why we do what we do
at mutter, we set out to hand-craft a solution to a problem that we personally faced, forgetting.
too many times my fiance and I found ourselves remembering things we had to do, but couldn’t write them down. Whether it was our dentist appointment while we were driving, or or birthday card while we were studying, life got in the way of staying on top of everything.
That’s why I made mutter, a platform designed to keep you in your own loop.
We listen to what you say, and pick out things you might want to remember, giving them back to you in a task-list so that you never miss another deadline, appointment, or anniversary again.
